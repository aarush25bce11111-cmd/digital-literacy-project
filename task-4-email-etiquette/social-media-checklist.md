## **Do’s**



* ###### Think before you post\*\* – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
* ###### Protect your privacy\*\* – Adjust privacy settings and limit personal information shared.
* ###### Verify information\*\* – Check facts before sharing news or posts.
* ###### Maintain a professional image\*\* – Remember that future employers may view your profile.
* ###### Be respectful and kind\*\* – Engage in positive and constructive interactions.



## **Don’ts**



* ###### Don’t share sensitive personal details\*\* – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or passwords.
* ###### Don’t spread misinformation\*\* – Avoid sharing unverified or fake content.
* ###### Don’t engage in online arguments or bullying\*\* – It can harm your reputation and others.
* ###### Don’t post inappropriate content\*\* – Offensive or explicit posts can have long-term consequences.
* ###### Don’t overshare your daily life\*\* – Too much information can compromise your safety and privacy.
