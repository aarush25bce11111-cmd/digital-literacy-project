### **'Stay Safe Online' checklist**





* ###### Use strong, unique passwords for each account (combine letters, numbers, symbols).
* ###### Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
* ###### Verify links and senders before clicking—especially emails claiming to be from colleges, banks, or job portals.
* ###### Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
* ###### Use secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into important accounts on public Wi-Fi.
* ###### Review app permissions before installing apps—only allow what is necessary.
* ###### Log out from shared devices (college labs, cyber cafés) after use.
* ###### Think before posting—avoid sharing personal details like ID cards, location, or travel plans.
* ###### Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
* ###### Always verify payment requests—remember, you enter your UPI PIN only to send money, not to receive it.
* ###### Use official apps only like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.
* ###### Avoid scanning unknown QR codes—they can lead to fraud or unauthorized payments.
* ###### Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
* ###### Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (especially for financial fraud—quick action can help recover money)
